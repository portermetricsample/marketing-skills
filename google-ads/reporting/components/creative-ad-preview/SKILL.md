# Google Ads — Creative Ad Preview

Renders one Responsive Search Ad as it appears in Google search — layout is the component, the "Google ad" skin lives in porter-design.

- **Files & usage:** [`README.md`](README.md)
- **Live preview:** [`demo.html`](demo.html)
- **Sample data:** `example.data.js` (fictional Acme Insurance)

Appearance is owned by the public **porter-design** repo (`portermetricsample/porter-design`), including the `CreativeAdPreview` skin; the component ships no CSS. Data comes from the Porter Metrics `google-ads` connector.
